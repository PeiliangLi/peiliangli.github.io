About

I am Peiliang Li, a second year Ph.D student in HKUST UAV Group at the Robotics Institute of Hong Kong University of Science and Technology supervised by Prof. Shaojie Shen . I am one of the authors of VINS-Mobile and VINS-Mono, currently the most porpular open-sourced VIO system. During my first Ph.D year, I mainly focus on visual inertial state estimation and its application (UAV navigation, Augmented Reality). After that I employ the 3D geometry methods inspired from VINS to solve the 3D object and ego-motion tracking problem. I received my B.Eng. degree in electrical science and technology from USTC , Hefei, China.

I'm interested in visual inertial fusion, 3D Computer Vision and Machine Learning with perticular looking for their combination to solve the 3D objects detection, tracking and ego-motion estimation for autonomous driving.
